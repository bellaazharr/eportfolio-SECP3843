# Overall Course Reflection — SECP3213

## What I Gained
This course shifted how I think about data work: a clean dashboard means nothing without a recommendation attached to it. Working on Phase 3 of the SkinAqua project specifically taught me to trace every recommendation back to a concrete pattern in the data, rather than writing the kind of safe, generic suggestion that sounds reasonable but isn't actually justified by anything I'd seen.

## Suggested Improvement
I'd benefit from earlier visibility into the data preparation stage (Alteryx workflows), not just the output. Several times I had to infer why a metric was calculated a certain way instead of knowing directly, which made writing confident recommendations harder than it needed to be.

## Personal Note
This course made me realize that the "BI" skillset I actually want to build isn't dashboard design — it's the judgment to turn a chart into a decision.
